Our Story

Eco Warriors volunteer club was formed in late 2022 by our three passionate founders who wanted to help the mauritian population to turn their compassion into action for a good cause and positve changes around them. Our paradise island has been facing major consequences regarding both the environment and the society and we are here to help each person work as a team and act together for a better future for Mauritius.

Our mission

Our mission consists mainly in raising awareness on important issues that prevail in our society nowadays. We help our members and the population as a whole to be understanding of their surroundings and to engage themselves in helping the environment and the less privileged persons along with providing them with a platform to meet new people and discover different cultures, staying physically fit and building their team work and communication skills.

Our team

All three founders of our club are Data Science students attending the University of Mauritius and University of CY Cergy-Paris, and they are currently in the third year of their course. The members of Eco Warriors club consists of people from all ages ranging from 15 to 65 years old and from different cultural as well as economic background who are all connected via their shared interest and compassion to assist in a good cause for a positive change.
