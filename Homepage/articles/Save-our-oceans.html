Save Our Oceans!

Updated : Thur, 12 Jan 2023 | By Oomar Ghingut | Environment

The ocean is one of the most vast and diverse places on our planet and our environment has been suffering immensely due to ocean pollution for the past decade. The amount of litter found in our water bodies threatens the marine environment at a very alarming rate.

According to environmentalists, more than 800 animal species are affected by ocean pollution worldwide and this figure is estimated to be around 23% more than it was five years ago. The massive amount of trash found on our beaches and coastal environment contributes to a large extent for this damaged condition of our Ocean. This is why it is of upmost importance that we keep our beaches clean and protect our ocean and environment.

Eco warriors volunteer club is organizing a beach cleaning event as from 25th to 28th February whereby we will be present on the most popular beaches of Mauritius : Blue Bay, Flic en Flac, Mont Choisy and Belle Mare to collect as much trash as possible for a better and cleaner future.

Register to our club for more details on upcoming events and help us take a step forward in saving the Sea for a better future.
