Health is Wealth !

Updated : Thur, 05 Jan 2023 | By Kholeepa Sadiyah | Health and Fitness

Health is Wealth is a term that was invented by the American philosopher Ralph Waldo Emerson in 1860. It is a phrase which is often used to designate that if a person has good health, he has almost everything and without it, wealth is just a fragile concept.

Nowadays, we live in an era whereby everyone's top priority is to grow in every aspect of life and amidst our modern lifestyles, health is something that is often overlooked and not valued enough by many persons. There are many factors which contribute in maintaining good and healthy lifestyles mainly: eating a healthy and balanced diet, avoid smoking and drinking alcohol, drinking a lot of water to stay hydrated and most importantly is to be active via regular exercises and physical activities.

Importance of exercise

Regular physical activities is one of the most important things a person can do for not only his physical wellbeing but also his mental and emotional health since exercise has been proven to have many benefits such as:

  1. More Energy
  2. Good night's sleep
  3. More productive
  4. More confidence and less stress
  5. Lower risk of cardiovascular diseases and cancer

Types of physical activities to stay healthy

Different ways to engage our body and mind and to keep us in good shape include:

  • Walking, running, jogging
  • Dancing, Zumba class
  • Hiking
  • Cycling
  • Swimming
  • Aerobic Class, Yoga
  • Going to the gym, Lifting weights
  • Pilates
  • Karate, Jiu jitsu
