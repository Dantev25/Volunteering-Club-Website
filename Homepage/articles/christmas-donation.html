Help bring a smile for christmas.

Updated : 12 Jan 2023 | By Rajanah Mrishana | Charity Events

While Christmas is the festive season of family reunion, beautiful decorations, tasty food and happy music, for some underprivileged families, elderly people and orphans, it might not be as delightful due to their financial or even family conditions. This period is a great time for us to be grateful for this wonderful year and the amazing opportunities that we got and to also help others and give something back.

Eco warriors club is organizing a charitable fund this year to collect donations for these underprivileged families so that we can bring some joy and light into their Christmas. Donors are invited to donate gifts, clothes, money, school materials, grocery and so on. Our team will be present at Bagatelle Mall, Jumbo Pheonix, Trainon Shopping Mall and at our club location from 16th to 23rd December to collect the funds.

Everyone is invited to contribute in making this Christmas a memorable one for these people.

Enjoy our all time favourite christmas song , 'Jingle Bells' in honor of this delightful festive season ;)

Even small gifts can collectively make a big difference to the lives of others. There are still plenty of ways you can give to the causes close to your heart, whatever your budget.
